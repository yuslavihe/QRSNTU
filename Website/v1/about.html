About QRS@NTU

Our Vision

A small, elite academic society at NTU dedicated to advancing quantitative research, fostering intellectual collaboration, and developing the next generation of leaders in mathematics and finance.

Core Focus Areas

Academic Research

Conduct original research across mathematics, economics, finance, and computational sciences. Collaborate on papers, competitions, and publications.

Specialised Learning

Organise advanced workshops and mini-courses. Provide a platform for undergraduates to engage with graduate-level theory.

Knowledge Sharing

Publish notes, guides, and problem sets for NTU courses. Build open resources for advanced quantitative learning.

Outreach & Impact

Host seminars, talks, and reading groups. Encourage interdisciplinary applications in finance, data science, and operations research.

Membership Structure

Our society accommodates varying levels of engagement and expertise, fostering both deep research and broad learning.

  • Core Researchers: Lead and actively contribute to research projects.
  • Associates: Participate in workshops, learning groups, and society activities.
  • Advisors: Faculty and professionals offering guidance and mentorship.
