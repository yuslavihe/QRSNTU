Events & Learning

Advanced workshops, mini-courses, seminars, and reading groups.

Planned Workshops

Semester 1: Core Foundations

Advanced Linear Algebra

Spectral theory, SVD, quadratic forms, semidefinite matrices.

Real Analysis (Introductory)

Limits, continuity, uniform convergence, compactness.

Probability Theory (Measure-Theoretic)

Probability spaces, expectation as Lebesgue integral, conditional expectation.

Semester 2: Measure, Integration & Stochastic Foundations

Measure Theory

σ-algebras, measurable functions, Lebesgue measure.

Integration Theory

Lebesgue vs. Riemann, Lebesgue–Stieltjes, convergence theorems.

Stochastic Processes (Intro)

Martingales, Markov chains, Brownian motion.

Semester 3: Functional Spaces & Analysis

Lp Spaces

Norms, completeness, Hölder/Minkowski inequalities.

Functional Analysis (Intro)

Banach/Hilbert spaces, Riesz representation, duality, weak/strong convergence.

Stochastic Calculus (Core)

Itô integrals, Itô’s lemma, stopping times, Girsanov theorem.

Semester 4: Advanced Methods (Optimization, Control, PDEs)

Convex Optimization

Duality, KKT, separation theorems.

Variational Calculus

Euler–Lagrange, functional minimization.

Optimal Control Theory

Pontryagin Maximum Principle, HJB equations.

Partial Differential Equations (PDEs)

Elliptic/parabolic PDEs, weak solutions, Sobolev spaces.
